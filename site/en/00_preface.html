Preface

Three months ago, my Agent team fell apart.

Not in some elegant, system-error kind of way — it was total chaos. Four Agents fought over the same task at once. The memory system mashed yesterday's conclusions together with today's requirements. And the Inspector just sat there like a decorative ornament while they passed the blame back and forth. Staring at the screen, I didn't feel like a boss. I felt like a firefighter surrounded by a squad of "digital interns."

That was my real experience over the past quarter.

It started with a bold idea — "Why not build an AI Agent team?" — and ran through the first thrill of "they're actually doing the work," then the crash, the post-mortem, the rebuild, and finally the stable, value-producing Agent team I have today. There was no magic in that journey. Just a great deal of trial and error, arguments (arguing with Agents sounds absurd, but it genuinely happens), and the patience to piece scattered workflows back together, again and again.

What this book wants to do is tell that story in a way you can actually follow.

I wrote it with a humanized, story-driven style. You'll meet "Kai" — the Agent who's always overconfident; you'll get to know "Max" — the Agent with a phenomenal memory that occasionally mixes things up. You'll see how they learn to collaborate under the Inspector's watchful eye, how they find their place in a goal-oriented framework, and how they repair themselves after a crash. The names are fictional, but the governance logic, the management methods, and the pitfalls behind the stories are all real.

The value of an Agent team isn't in how many people it replaces — it's in the new possibilities it opens up for you. When your Agent team truly works, you'll find yourself shifting from doer to commander. Your time gets freed up to spend on the things that genuinely require human judgment.

This book won't promise your Agent team will never crash. But it will tell you how to rebuild after a crash, how to govern through the chaos, and — ultimately — how to keep your Agent team creating value for you, continuously.

Ready? Let's meet the team.

— Yason
